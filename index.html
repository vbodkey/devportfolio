About Me

With over 8 years of IT experience, I am a seasoned Full Stack .Net Developer proficient in all phases of the Software Development Life Cycle. Demonstrating expertise in designing and developing web services and applications using object-oriented methodologies, I excel in the .NET framework and C# programming, creating well-structured and maintainable code. Skilled in front-end development with JavaScript, I enhance user interfaces for an optimal experience. My extensive knowledge encompasses database design, optimization, and management, with hands-on experience in SQL and Oracle databases. Well-versed in both front-end and back-end development, I provide end-to-end solutions tailored to industry standards. Specializing in life and annuity applications, I ensure compliance with regulations and standards. Proficient in XML technologies, debugging, and solving production issues, I guarantee the robust and reliable performance of insurance applications. My skills extend to optimizing database performance, analyzing application requirements, and designing normalized database schemas. With excellent written and verbal communication skills, analytical prowess, problem-solving abilities, and attention to detail, I thrive both independently and as a team leader within collaborative environments.
